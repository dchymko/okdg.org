April 2013 — April 23rd 2013

Embracing the Z-axis: 3D printers, CNC machines, laser cutters and you by Chad Kohalyk

Within the last decade digital fabrication technology has revolutionized personal manufacturing, giving rise to the Maker Movement. Chad Kohalyk reports on his trip to Makerhaus – a 10,000 ft2 maker facility in Seattle – and discusses how this coming technology revolution will change society.

Tales of The Steamship Vol 2. by Ryan Lahay

Last time Ryan was at OKDG, he talked about the some of the benefits designing for "mobile first". Now he is back to expand on the topic and share his workflow for creating content driven websites across a wide array of devices using responsive design techniques.

The Bike Shop Cafe and Catering Co. has warmly welcomed us back to use their restaurant as a meeting venue. There will be food, beverages and alcohol available throughout the evening.
