January 2014 — January 22nd 2014

Big quality from small teams by Daryl Chymko

Through case studies from the startups he's been involved with, Daryl will show how small dev teams can deliver a great user experience that years ago was only possibly with large technical teams.

Pursuing your passion project by Christian Brandt

After having finished his Master's degree in Environmental Management and Policy in Sweden, Christian moved to Kelowna with his partner where he started working on establishing the Okanagan Car Share Co-op. His talk will elaborate more about why he decided to stay in Kelowna despite their plans to stay only for a couple of months in BC and why he was driven particular to this project. He will share some tips on how to pursue a passion project which might inspire more people to move from just having an idea to doing it.

The Bike Shop Cafe and Catering Co. has warmly welcomed us back to use their restaurant as a meeting venue. There will be food, beverages and alcohol available throughout the evening.
