Movember — November 20th 2012

Teach Your Browser New Tricks by Brett LaForge

Building smarter, data-bound Javascript Web Applications with Angular.js

Making a living through your own ideas by Todd Ramsay

Using colourful language, his twisted sense of humor, as well as his own successes and challenges, Todd will talk about finding work that that supports your personal style.

The Evening

Brett Laforge of Vineyard Networks will start us off with a talk titled "Teaching your browser new tricks." In his presentation Brett will show how to write smarter client-side web apps with Angular.js. Web developers won't want to miss this one.

Next, Todd Ramsay of Yeti Farm Creative will shift gears and talk about his trials and tribulations in trying to make a living creating his own ideas rather than doing service work. Hilarious stories, facial expressions and potty language is guaranteed at this talk.

The Bike Shop Cafe and Catering Co. has warmly welcomed us back to use their restaurant as a meeting venue. There will be food and your ‘beverages of your choice’ available throughout the evening.

Looking forward to seeing you all there!
Shane and Daryl

Please note the doors open at at 7:30pm and presentations will start at 8:00pm sharp. Please do not show up too early before 7:30pm as we are setting up the room and dealing with facilities.
