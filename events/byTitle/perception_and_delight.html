Perception and Delight — May 25th 2010

User Experience + Delight by Aaron DeSimone

Aaron discusses the importance of user experience on the internet. Topics covered include: What is user experience? What makes a good experience? and Delight : the difference between good and great experiences.

Perception by Lance Priebe

Lance Priebe discusses what the client percieves vs. what is actually happening. He also dives into how it is more important to design from the users perspective.

After a two month hiatus we are incredibly excited for this evening's meetup. We have two presenters coming to us from Disney Online Studios Canada.

Aaron is opening the evening discussing User Experience + Delight which will be an interesting topic for all front end developers and designers

Lance is ending the evening with a short presentation on Perception and then opening the floor for a question and answer period.

Check back for slides and a video recording of the night.
