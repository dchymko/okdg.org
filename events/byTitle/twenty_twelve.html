Kicking Off Twenty Twelve — January 31st 2012

Writing for the Web by Anne-Rachelle McHugh

Anne-Rachelle McHugh will discuss the much-misunderstood world of web writing – focusing on what web designers can do to ensure writing compliments – rather than distracts from – their work.

Versioning + Process by Adrian Schneider

Source control, and more importantly, well-defined process, is something that most small development shops overlook. Adrian will go over how to get started with source control, and some different workflows or processes which help improve the quality of your development efforts.

The Evening

It's a new year and we've got another exciting event with two awesome speakers lined up! Come out on the 31th to network with other great folks in the technology and design fields

The Bike Shop Cafe and Catering Co. has warmly welcomed us back to use their restaurant as a meeting venue. There will be food, beverages and alcohol available throughout the evening.

Looking forward to seeing you all there!

Shane & Daryl
