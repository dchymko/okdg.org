Unreal HTML — October 26th 2010

HTML5 And You by Dustin Senos

Dustin dives into what HTML5 is, and how you can use it to your advantage on your next project.

Unreal Engine by Brent Luehr

Brent explains the brief history of the Unreal Engine, and how you can take advantage of it to make games easily.

New Venue

We're happy to announce that we have lined up a venue for the October 26th event. This month's meetup (and hopefully all to come) will be held at the Bike Shop Cafe and Catering Co. There will be food and alcohol available at the venue. We're really looking forward to this venue as it is locally owned and operated, in a great location, and the owners are incredibly nice people who have stepped up to help out OKDG.

Please note the doors open at at 7:30pm and presentations will start at 8:00pm sharp. Please do not show up too early before 7:30pm as we are setting up the room and dealing with facilities.

The Evening

The evening is starting off with Dustin Senos discussing what HTML5 is, what it isn't, and how it can be used in today's development.

Following Dustin's presentation Brent Luehr will be presenting the Unreal Engine and some fun uses.

Looking forward to seeing you all there!
Shane, Derrick and Dustin
