Christmas 2012 — December 19th 2012

Artist Inspiration by Jessika vonInnerebner

Cale Atkinson of Hyper Hippo productions and Jessika von Innerebner of, "I have too many projects to list" will be covering the skills you need to survive in the art/animation industry. This duo will go through topics from inspiration to the importance of networking and how you always want to keep pencil to paper. Bring your questions too!

Highlights from Boulder by Jeff Keen

Jeff Keen of Accelerate Okanagan will give highlights from his recent visit to the startup community in Boudler, CO. If you're a budding enterpreneur or startup, you won't want to miss this.

2012 Re-cap by Shane Austin

Daryl Chymko and Shane Austin will be wrapping up 2012 at OKDG with stories of the past year, then revealing some big plans and exciting events for the new year.

The Evening

The Bike Shop Cafe and Catering Co. has warmly welcomed us back to use their restaurant as a meeting venue. There will be food and your ‘beverages of your choice’ available throughout the evening.

Cale Atkinson of Hyper Hippo productions and Jessika von Innerebner of, "I have too many projects to list Inc." will start us off by covering the skills you need to survive in the art/animation industry. This duo will go through topics from inspiration to the importance of networking and how you always want to keep pencil to paper. Bring your questions too!

Next, Daryl Chymko and Shane Austin will be wrapping up 2012 at OKDG with stories of the past year, then revealing some big plans and exciting events for the new year.

Looking forward to seeing you all there!
Shane and Daryl

Please note the doors open at at 7:30pm and presentations will start at 8:00pm sharp. Please do not show up too early before 7:30pm as we are setting up the room and dealing with facilities.
