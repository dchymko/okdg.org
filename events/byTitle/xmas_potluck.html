Tis the season and what better way to celebrate than with generous portions of good food and great people? The members of co+Lab have opened their doors to all OKDG’ers for a potluck-style evening on the Friday following the Ignite night.

There will be plenty of food on hand, and we hear there may even be a special “co+Lab” beer ready by then. You can bring whatever dish you like but please let us know ahead of time by RSVPing and adding your food contribution on the sign-up sheet
