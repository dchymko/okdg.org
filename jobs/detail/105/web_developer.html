Web Developer

Roketto in Kelowna, BC, Canada

About Us

Roketto is a digital agency focused on helping companies strengthen their brand and connect with their audience. Our sweet spots are responsive web design, results based internet marketing, and branding.
We are young professionals who are looking to grow our team to help us continue creating and marketing great websites.


Position Description

As a web developer for Roketto you will be given the task of developing new websites, as well as maintaining and updating existing client websites. These tasks will require you to be comfortable coding in HTML5, CSS, Javascript and PHP.

Most of your work will involve customizing, updating, and building websites on Concrete5 or WordPress. Experience and knowledge of these platforms is extremely valuable.

We are looking for someone with minimum 1 year experience in the field who can hit the ground running. Training will be minimal as we need someone to help us out immediately with our current projects. You should enjoy solving problems independently and keeping up to date with the newest and most effective methods of web development.

Qualifications

- HTML(5), CSS, JavaScript + jQuery, PHP
- Basic understand of importing & exporting MySQL databases
- Basic Linux command line
- Setting up CMS websites from scratch
- Experience with media queries and building responsive websites
- Excellent at solving the challenges of cross browser rendering (desktop [IE, FF, Chrome, Safari] and mobile[iOS, Android])

Bonus Skills

- SASS
- JSON, AJAX
- Experience with GIT


Additional Information

Key Traits

- PASSIONATE about evolving web development technologies including JavaScript frameworks and HTML5
- Excellent communications skills
- Strong analytic and problem-solving skills
- Superior teamwork and interpersonal skills
- Effective time management skills

Education

- A college or university degree in something technical is preferred; your experience also speaks to what you didn't learn in a classroom. 
- We are also open if you can demonstrate you know your stuff and have a non-conventional degree.

Working Conditions

- Downtown Kelowna office location
- 27" iMac w/ OS X
- Dynamic work environment
- Coffee, tea, snacks provided
