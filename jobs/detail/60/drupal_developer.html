Drupal Developer

Cheeky Monkey Media in anywhere

Cheeky Monkey Media is looking for a Contract / Remote Drupal Developer. In order to apply for this position, you must meet the following requirements. CMM is looking for someone who is self motivated, responsible and enjoys working in a team setting. It’s important that the person joining CMM knows and understands Drupal to the fullest extent. As a team member, you will work closely with other team members and also with a large clientele base. The person chosen for this position will work with a set budget, and you will be required to be available during normal business hours. This is important, because you will need to interact with the rest of the team while completing various projects.

Roles & Responsibilities
·         Develop solutions using Drupal 6 and 7
·         Complete various tasks as requested by supervisor.
·         Work in a team setting
·         Contribute ideas frequently to help solve problems
·         Troubleshoot and test various websites/Take care of technical problems as they arise
·         Other responsibilities & roles may be involved but that will be discussed upon contact with Cheeky Monkey Media.

Qualifications
·         Experience using GIT
·         Knowledge of core concepts of Drupal platform
·         Experience developing custom modules and integrating with community contributed
·         Strong skills in PHP, HTML and Javascript
·         Prefer 2+ years of Drupal Development
·         Experience with WordPress a plus
·         Writes and Speaks English

Preferred Characteristics & Qualifications
·         Self motivated
·         Positive attitude
·         Based in the United States or Canada
·         Work alone and in a team setting
·         Highly Communicative
·         Enjoys a good challenge
·         Remote worker
·         Available during normal business hours

Compensation
The rates offered by Cheeky Monkey Media are competitive. Compensation will vary depending on your expertise, education and experience.

About Cheeky Monkey Media
Cheeky Monkey Media is a web design business that strives to please its customers. We like to think outside of the cage and help improve websites. CMM is located in Kelowna, BC and is more than just a website development company. We are laid back monkeys that like to have fun, but also know when it’s time to get down to monkey business.
