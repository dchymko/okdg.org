iOS Developer

Bawtree Software Contracting Ltd in Salmon Arm

We are looking for a highly motivated and talented person to join our iOS development team. A qualified applicant will be familiar with Apple's iOS development SDK, XCode, and have a solid understanding of developing for the mobile platform.

Minimum Requirements 

  • 3+ years experience in Objective-C, Java, C++ or other high-level OOP language.
  • Minimum 1 year experience developing for a mobile platform, including iOS, Android, or Blackberry.
  • Bachelor's degree in software development.
  • Curious and eager to learn about mobile technologies.

 

Key Qualifications 

  • Has an app on the App Store or has worked with a team who has.
  • Capable of pair programming to learn new systems and diagnose and solve complex problems.
  • Working knowledge of source control (Git, Mercurial, Subversion, Perforce or other) and issue tracking systems.
  • Familiar with Apple's iOS & Mac operating systems (Linux or UNIX experience is helpful).

 

Nice to Have's 

  • Knows their way around Photoshop and can create basic graphic elements.
  • Experience with web standards, including HTML & CSS.
  • Familiar with Agile programming and Scrum.

 

Ideal candidate 

  • Smart and humble - always learning from your colleagues!
  • Understanding of Object Oriented Design Patterns and how to apply them to Objective-C
  • Articulate - capable of distilling complex technical issues into questions that non-technical people can answer.
  • A good listener - sometimes this is difficult when you are an expert. Can you do it?
  • A good writer - can communicate clearly and effectively with people who have a range of technical expertise.

How to Apply

Send your resume to info@bawtreesoftware.com or connect with us via LinkedIn.
