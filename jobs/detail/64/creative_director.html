Creative Director

ROX Brand in Kelowna, BC


ROX Brand Inc. is a results-driven strategic agency. Through a unique consultative and collaborative approach we thrive on building inspired organizational strategy and evocative creative that gets business results. We create extraordinarily successful brands because we challenge long held assumptions and dare our clients to dream bigger, and give them the tools to meet their goals.
Summary of Position
The Creative Director at ROX must be versatile - able to think strategically, conceptually and creatively; from developing a creative strategy all the way through to creating a comprehensive design platform and wide range of deliverables. The ideal candidate will have an incredible eye for detail, astute design skills and the ability to effectively communicate and lead interdisciplinary teams. He or she will have the energy to inspire, the experience to lead, and the ability to produce excellent creative results; work in close collaboration with strategists and other creative’s to ensure client design efforts fall in line with brand guidelines across all channels (collateral, advertising, online, retail, etc.)
Specific Responsibilities

  • Attend and co-facilitate client business and brand planning sessions
  • Work with ROX strategists in evaluating objectives and develop brand platform, creative concepts and design solutions that align with business strategy
  • Consult with creative and web team to ensure all deliverables meet or exceed business objectives, brand guidelines and creative briefs 
  • Top level design and creative direction of key communication pieces of a brand
  • Direct and lead junior and intermediate designers
  • Effectively communicate and present design concepts/ strategic direction
  • Stay current on design style, knowledge and trends
  • Some business development support work will be required

 Technical Qualifications
  • 5+ years of design and conceptual experience 
  • Strong collaboration and communication skills
  • Ability to work well as a team player but demonstrates skills as a leader 
  • Possesses excellent time management skills, a love of design, knowledge and interest in taking strategy through concept to final execution of artwork
  • Must understand working within existing brand framework, building and adhering to brand standards
  • Possesses strong working knowledge of typography, layout, color, and visual communications
  • Mastery of the Adobe Creative Suite and other design platforms
  • Understanding of current web technologies and design
  • Experience working closely with cross-functional teams 
  • Works well under pressure and with deadlines
  • Strong attention to detail

Salary, Perks and Benefits
  • Exciting, progressive energy, working with a tight team of professionals
  • Competitive pay based on skills and experience
  • Office outings, travel and professional development opportunities
  • Opportunity to live and work in the sunny Okanagan, Kelowna, BC
  • Casual dress, flexible work hours

How to Apply

Interested candidates are asked to email their resume as well as portfolio URL or PDF to careers@roxbrand.ca. No telephone calls please.
