Full Time Systems Engineer

TEC - Centre for Arts and Technology in Kelowna, BC, Canada

The Education Company (TEC), was established in 2003 and runs specialized educational campuses across Canada, including Stenberg College, Centre for Health Studies, and Centre for Arts and Technology. We are currently looking for candidates for the full time position of Systems Engineer at TEC, to be based out of our Centre for Arts and Technology Kelowna campus.

Centre for Arts and Technology is an accredited, higher education institution providing specialized technical training and sought after skills in a rapidly expanding and evolving entertainment arts and media production industry.

Objective/Purpose of the Position:
• To provide technical expertise and skills for the management, support and development of the IT estate.

Key Responsibilities of the Position:
• Provide 2nd/3rd tier technical support and advice to end users of 80% Wintel / 20% Mac environment.
• Lead or assist the research, implementation and handover of both business and operational IS projects.

Education and Experience Preferred
Successful candidates will have at least 80% of these:

• Excellent Troubleshooting skills
• Microsoft Active Directory Infrastructure including DNS, GPO’s, DFS, domains, forests, trusts
• Microsoft core server products including SPPS/MOSS, ISA, SQL Server
• Experience with Cisco Networking & Infrastructure
• Excellent Windows 7 diagnostic skills.
• MS Office 2007/2010
• MCSE/MCITP certification
• Familiar with Virtualization technologies
• Understanding of basic networking concepts, LAN, WAN, VLAN, Subnet.

Desirable but not essential:

• BES
• Cisco ASA
• IPSEC VPN
• CCNA/CCNP
• OSX Server; MCX policies, open directory (LDAP)
• Linux: postfix, BIND, SAMBA, Asterisk
• Microsoft PKI, SCCM (or MDT2010), GP (Dynamics), Exchange 2007/10, Visio
• Software packaging / MSI customization, answer files etc.
• Experience programming in scripted or compiled languages (shell/perl/php/VB/JS/powershell/applescript)

Along with a competitive salary and benefits package we offer you the opportunity to grow your career with one of the most dynamic private colleges in Canada. 

How to Apply

Please send your cover letter and resume to ithr@tecglobal.net. Only candidates selected for an interview will be contacted. NO PHONE CALLS PLEASE!
