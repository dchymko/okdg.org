Web Developer/Programmer

Csek Creative in Kelowna

Looking to bring another member into the team at Csek Creative

 

What we are looking for a candidate to bring to the table

·      Minimum 3-5 years of experience developing LAMP based web applications in both procedural and OOP programming styles

·      Writing standards-compliant CSS, HTML, and JavaScript

·      Good understanding of Apache web server configuration and Linux administration skills

·      Quick learner - you're comfortable digging into legacy systems and hacking existing code

·      Ability to prioritize and work well under pressure;

·      Strong communication skills (verbal and written)

·      Strong customer service skills

·      Excellent interpersonal skills

·      Strong organizational, prioritization and time management skills

·      A demonstrated ability to work independently and in a team setting

·      The ability to effectively handle multiple tasks and projects and to meet deadlines

·      A positive, professional attitude and demeanor

 

What we bring to the table

·      A competitive compensation based of experience

·      Unique office environment in downtown Kelowna Cultural District

·      The opportunity to work within a portfolio of over 700 clients

·      A great team of ten like-minded individuals that eat/breathe/sleep web

·      Opportunity for growth in a fast moving organization

 

About Csek Creative

Csek Creative and DotCom Media have been serving the needs of over 700 Okanagan based businesses for over 13 years now. They are one of Kelowna's oldest digital agencies and are located in the heart of downtown Kelowna.

How to Apply

Please include a portfolio that best demonstrates your range in technical abilities implementation of websites careers@csekcreative.com. No phone calls please. Only selected applicants will be contacted. Thank you.
