Javascript / Node Developer

Just Be Friends Kids Inc. in Kelowna, BC

Just Be Friends Network is developing a safe and fun online network for families, helping them find and form quality friendships for their children. We’re a fast moving company of extremely passionate people that believe in strong corporate ethics and realize that a fun and creative atmosphere makes for happier employees and a better experience for end users.

We’re looking to take our MVP to the next level and need a bigger team to make this happen! 

 

If you feel comfortable with most of these things, please get in touch:

  •  Strong knowledge of Javascript, specifically experience with node.js
    •  Express Framework
    • Passport / OAuth
  • Understanding of RESTful interfaces and AJAX
  • Knowledge of security best practices (sanitizing user input, etc)
    • Cryptology experience a bonus (password hashing/salting, encryption)
  • Experience working with payment processors/gateways
  • Testing frameworks (Jasmine, Nodeunit, Zombie, PhantomJS, etc)
  • GIT for source control
  • DevOps experience a bonus

 

Some perks of the job:

  • A brand new office where you can decorate your work environment!
  • A ball pit
  • A stellar team that encourages creativity and respects your input
  • Access to caffeine in large quantities
  • Feel good about what you’re doing, you’re helping change the world!
  • Awesome title and cartoon on your business card
